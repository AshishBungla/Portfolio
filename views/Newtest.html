Microsoft Design Challenge 2020

The project aims to create a free learning community that focuses on learning through knowledge sharing among its members and providing new interaction opportunities and promote senior-peer learning.

Overview

Introduction

This project was a submission for a competition called Microsoft Design Challenge 2020. The competition aims to find digital solutions to problems in the field of learning, health and transportation in the context of the Covid-19 Pandemic.

For the competition, I have chosen Education & Learning as the theme.

Problem Statement

Most of the platform that gives free learning content(Youtube, Facebook) has many distractions while studying. The reason is simple because they are not created for learning purposes. Other platforms that allow good learning content are mostly paid and hence not accessible by everyone

There is a need for learning platforms that are freely accessible, allow diverse learning content without any distractions. These platforms also need to consider socialising issues that students face in the current pandemic scenario.

Problem Timeline

Target Audience

The target group for this platform are students from class 9th standard and above who want to learn online regarding any topic, chapter or field without any substantial subscription charge.

User Research

Primary Research

This research includes a survey of 100 students residing in different cities and age groups to understand their online learning perspective. The questionnaire was designed to understand the current online learning platforms and preferences of students .


Questionnaire

  • What is the average time per day that you spend on screen excluding your online classes?
  • The average time per day that you spend on screen for learning something excluding online classes?
  • If you want to learn something online(chapter/concept), how will you arrange the following learning media in your preference? (Teachers, Seniors/Peers, Free learning Platforms, Paid Education Platforms)
  • If you need help in learning online or doubts, how will you arrange the following learning media in your preference? (Teachers, Seniors/Peers, Free learning Platforms, Paid Education Platforms)
  • What are the problems they face during online classes?
  • Do they face hesitation when asking doubts from teachers ?
  • How much money they can spend on online learning platform in a month?
  • Do you think you can teach any chapter or concept to your junior students ?

Key Insights

  1. Students preferred free learning platforms (generally youtube) more than their teachers due to more diverse learning options.
  2. When they need to understand doubts or concept, they preferred their peers and seniors rather than teachers.
  3. Unless needed professional certificates, students choose free platforms rather than paid educational apps.
  4. Many students feel judged while asking doubts from teachers hence prefers their peers/seniors.
  5. Learning is limited to academics from teachers and paid educational platforms, Free platform like youtube provide a large variety to learn online.

User Stories

I extracted two major user stories from my primary research and highlighted ther frustations and desires.

User Needs Identified

The student needs a learning environment with

  • Reduced Distraction i.e an education-focused platform, unlike Youtube/Facebook.
  • Easy accessibility and availability of teachers and Classes.
  • Different Options for learning and clarifying doubts.
  • Good possibilities of social interaction that can enhance peer learning.
  • Option to learn dance, music or other art rather than just academics.

Ideation

Design Concept

The idea is to create a self suistaining education community. It is inspired from the concept of Time Banking. Time Banking is a system of bartering various services to one another using time spent as a unit of account.


Here, we are considering knowledge as a coummunity resource which can be shared between its members.





The member sharing his/her knowledge in the form of class will get credits in return which he/she can use either gaining knowledge from community or after reaching a threshold can convert into real money.

Key Ideas

Storyboarding

Working

When a member wishes to learn something, he/she can enroll in an upcoming class but if there is no such class on the platform, the user can post a request for that particular topic.



The user can also vote a related request to increase the chances of it being taken up by a suitable member as a tutor. The requests posted will remain for a fixed period of time after which the user will be notified that his request has not been accepted at the moment.


Approved Class Request

Once a user(tutor) approves a request, the class request will automatically be posted on class schedule feed and all the students who have voted for request will be enrolled for that class.

Classes or Courses Approval (for tutors)

User as a tutor can post a Class for any topic or even design a course for students. But educational courses first need to be first verified by community and then allowed for teaching.

Revenue Model


Subscription Fee

Most part of the community will be freely accessible, the subsription will only be required for exclusive classes organised by community. The subcription will benefit users for accessing one on one classes, exclusive online sessions/lectures etc.

Donation Tips

Members can donate money to teachers for any class they like. Some percentage of that money is will be sent off to community.

Earning Credits

Members can earn credits by hosting classes and by active participation on Discussion Forum. This credits at one point can be extracted in real money by its members.

Badges

Members will be provided with badges that will also be determined by number of classes hosted and students feedback. Badges will act as a sign of credibility and authenticity of that member. After earning certain Badges, user classes will become exclusive and thus get paid for classes.

Final Design

Final Interfaces

Home Feed

User lands on home feed page where he can see Scheduled upcoming classes and class request by other users.

As Student

  • If user wants a class on certain topic, he first search for that topic and see if there is any particular class or request on feed.
  • If user finds a class, then he enroll for it.
  • If user finds request for that class, he votes for it.

As Tutor

  • If user want to schedule a class for any topic, he/she checks other user’s requests and see if any matches with his/her interest.
  • If matches and has threshold votes from other users, user approves that request and schedule class on that request.
  • Else user schedules a class from post and his notice is seen in feed.

Your Schedule

As Student

  • User can see his/her enrolled classes and can join from there.
  • User cannot join class until 5 mins before the timing.
  • User can post any request from Create Post.

As Tutor

  • User can see his approved or posted classes and host class from there.
  • User can schedule any class notice fromCreate Post.

Community Space

Community space shows trending users and sub communties. Its shows all activities going on the community.

Trending Users

  • Users who are trending( most classes & students ) are displayed.
  • One can see there user ‘s profile.

Trending Communities

  • Groups who are trending( most number of activities & members) are displayed.
  • User can see community profile and join them if interested.

Sub Community

Sub Community/group includes post from community members that share same interests and domain.

  • User can post anything inside group and group member get notified.
  • User can see other member’s post.
  • User can see videos of classes held earlier.

Discussion Forum

  • This forum allows users to post any question or doubts and get answers from community.
  • Users can also see query asked by other members and reply the answers.
  • The queries are divided in 3 sections : Top, Latest, and Unanswered.
  • The answers in replies can be upvoted/downvoted to show its correctness.

Profile Section

  • Profile section includes user info and details like followers and following.
  • Credits earned by user by taking Classes.
  • Badges/Stars are shown that represent authenticity and experience of user.
  • User can see Notifications, Chats and Communities.
